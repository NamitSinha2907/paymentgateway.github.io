About

Mission:

The mission of this NGO is to ensure the upliftment of the poor and needy. We aim to bring different parts of the society together and give them a way to help the needy.

The organization started 3 decades ago with a vision to serve the needy and cure the helpless patients through free corrective surgeries with a will to fight a battle with Polio and other related birth disabilities. An internationally renowned center with more than 12 specialized hospitals, 1100+ beds, food for 4500+ people every day, and more than 426350 free of cost corrective surgeries; the Sansthan has become more like a family and home to all the patients and their visitors.

Basic needs for the children is knowledge and education. We aim to empower them with education.

  1. Providing them with schooling essentials.
  2. Mid-day meals for all students.
  3. Uniforms for students which helps them in indulging a sense of responsibility and uniformity in their life.

Vision

  2. Having connections with people who are willing to donate for a noble cause.
